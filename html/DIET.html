Description of DIET

Diets based on the use of naturally occurring products. This type of interventions targets biological effects, with interventions of a nutritional nature. The mechanisms involved could be related to modulation of the immune, antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, nervous (e.g. neuromodulation), or cognitive functioning systems. Encouraging natural improvement of somatic disorders (e.g. gastrointestinal), may have the additional effect of improving certain behavioral disorders (the well-being induced by physiological changes promoting general appeasement). Special diets or dietary supplements are the most frequently used CAMs. The expected effects of a restrictive diet rely on the removal of potential allergens.
