Description of PHYS

The physical activity discussed here is part of interventions designed to prevent mental or emotional difficulties.

Regular physical activity is associated with reduced anxiety and depression, improved self-image, and improved concentration, memory and academic performance. In the context of ASD, physical activity interventions are based on the assumption of improved mental health and behaviour in people with ASD.

Some authors highlight the frequency of anxiety, hyperactivity, sensory processing difficulties, which impact on the person's ability to function. According to them, physical activity interventions should result in the replacement of stereotypical behaviours with socially acceptable behaviours, and thus in a better quality of life.
