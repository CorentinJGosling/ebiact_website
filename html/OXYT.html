Description of OXYT

Interventions based on intranasal oxytocin (hormone) administration are based on the assumption of its involvement in social cognition (Hammock et al., 2015), including theory of mind and empathy. The underlying mechanisms could be fear reduction, increased social motivation, and improved detection of social cues.
